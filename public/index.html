Track Your Progress

Once you invest, your progress is tracked in real-time. You can see how much time is left and when your investment will be completed.

What is CyberToken (CYT)?

CyberToken (CYT) is the main unit used for investment in this system. Instead of investing directly with your wallet balance, you first convert your money into CYT and then use it for investment.

1 CYT = ₱500

For example, if you have ₱5,000, you can convert it into 10 CYT. You can then use those 10 CYT to start an investment plan.
